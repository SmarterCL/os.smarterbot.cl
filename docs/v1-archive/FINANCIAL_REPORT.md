### Market

**Target Market**: Ecommerce SMEs in Chile
- **Size**: ~2,500 active ecommerce businesses
- **Average Monthly Volume**: $50,000 - $500,000
- **Current Fee Rate**: 2.5% - 3.5%
- **Problem**: High fees kill margins (5-15% typical ecommerce margin)

**Solution Value**:
- Reduce effective fees from 3% → 1.9% = 37% savings
- Average merchant: $200,000/month × 1.1% savings = $2,200/month
- SmarterOS takes 35% = $770/month per merchant

**Go-to-Market**:
1. **Phase 1** (Month 1-3): 5-10 early adopters ($3,850 - $7,700 MRR)
2. **Phase 2** (Month 4-6): Partnerships with payment processors ($25,000 MRR)
3. **Phase 3** (Month 7-12): Ecosystem growth, ecosystem (100+ merchants, $77,000 MRR)
